Major Infrastructure Development Plan Announced

March 30, 2025

The South African government has unveiled a comprehensive R500 billion infrastructure development plan aimed at revitalizing key sectors of the economy and creating over 100,000 jobs in the next five years.

The plan focuses on transportation networks, energy infrastructure, water management systems, and digital connectivity across both urban and rural areas.
